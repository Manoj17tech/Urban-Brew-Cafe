FAQ

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery within a 5-mile radius through our website. Delivery fee is $3.99.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Add special instructions in the order details, and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer gluten-free bread and several gluten-free pastry options. Just specify in your order.

How long does pickup take?

Most orders are ready within 15-20 minutes. You'll receive a text when your order is ready.
